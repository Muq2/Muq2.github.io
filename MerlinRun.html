Merlin Run

  1. Projects
  2. Merlin Run

A fast paced side scrolling platform game.

  • Muqadam Sabir
  • Game Development
  • May 5, 2022

Step into a magical realm where wizardry meets adrenaline in the thrilling side-scrolling platform game - Merlin Run! Drawing inspiration from classics like Jetpack Joyride and the Chrome Dino game, this enchanting adventure combines the best of both worlds, promising an exhilarating experience for players of all ages.

Gameplay:
In Merlin Run, you embody the role of a skilled wizard who finds themselves in a race against time, fleeing from the ominous Demon King. The journey is fraught with challenges, and the key to survival lies in your ability to navigate through the treacherous terrain.

Features:
Audio Extravaganza:
Immerse yourself in the magical atmosphere with a captivating soundtrack that evolves dynamically as you progress through the game. Every step, jump, and spell cast is accompanied by enchanting melodies, enhancing the overall gaming experience.
Parallax Scrolling:
The visually stunning parallax scrolling adds depth to the mystical landscapes, making every backdrop a feast for the eyes. Traverse through mesmerizing environments that shift and change as you sprint towards safety.
Double Jump Mastery:
As a wizard, you possess the power of double jumping. Leap gracefully through the air, avoiding obstacles and reaching new heights. Mastering the double jump is crucial for outsmarting the Demon King's relentless pursuit.
Fast Fall Thrills: Gain the upper hand by utilizing the fast fall ability. Descend rapidly, dodging hazards with precision timing. The combination of double jumping and fast falling creates a dynamic gameplay experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat.
Animated Sprites:
Watch as your wizard comes to life with meticulously crafted animated sprites. From nimble jumps to magical spells, each movement is a visual spectacle, adding an extra layer of charm to the game.

Concept: The narrative behind Merlin Run is simple yet captivating - you're a wizard desperately escaping the clutches of the Demon King. The stakes are high, and every step must be calculated to avoid the perils that lie in wait. Tripping on a seemingly innocent rock could spell doom, adding an element of suspense to the already adrenaline-pumping adventure.

Merlin Run invites players into a world where magic and danger collide. With its compelling features, stunning visuals, and heart-pounding gameplay, this side-scrolling platformer promises an enchanting experience for those seeking thrills and excitement. Embark on a journey, test your wizardry skills, and see if you have what it takes to outrun the Demon King in Merlin Run!
